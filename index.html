Summary: Lattitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equater. To accomplish this analaysis, we first pulled data from the OpenWeatherApi to assemble a datset on over 500 cities.

After assembling the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot carious aspects of the weather vs. latitude Factors we looked at include: tempature, cludiness, wind speed, and huidity. This site provides the source data and visualizations created as part of the analysis, as well as explanations and descriptions of any trends and correlations witnessed.
